// 02  Transcription Modes

Three modes.
Switch mid-interview.

Pick the mode that fits the moment — or hot-swap on the fly. No restart required.

mode-01
Pulse
Real-Time Streaming

Words appear as you speak — ultra-low latency, word by word. Best for HR and behavioral rounds where you need answers the moment a question lands.

mode-02
Echo
High-Accuracy Batch

Waits for a natural pause and delivers a precise, complete transcript. Perfect for technical rounds where accuracy matters more than speed.

mode-03
Fusion
Best of Both

Live preview while speaking, upgraded to an accurate committed transcript on pause. Ideal for complex discussions where every word in the answer counts.

All modes work with both Clue AI and Clue Pro engines. Switch engines or modes mid-session with a single click — no restart needed.
